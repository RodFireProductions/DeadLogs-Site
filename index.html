DeadJournals has awaken! IT'S ALIVE!!!!

DeadJournals is a tool for making static blogs using html, css, and vanilla javascript. Minimum html markup and javascript array and dictionary knowledge is needed to begin working with DeadJournals.

If you need a little help getting started, there's a tutorial waiting for you!

-❁-❁-

What's the purpose?

Who needs a site creator with zero coding when you can get a site creator with minimum coding?

I suppose you could say DeadJournals is a waste of time with all the other static site generators and website builders out there, and you may be right. But isn't the idea of owning your site and being able to transport it anywhere without having to pay to download it or get the watermark removed pretty neat? Plus DeadJournals is merely a simplistic shell; if one has the skill and knowledge, then they can add any feature to their deadjournal they want. The sky is the limit! And for those who don't know much or don't need much, they're able to use DeadJournals as it comes with some possible tweaks.
