Images and Media

It might not matter in other contexts, but let me tell you: When it comes to web, game, and software development size does matter.

Files sizes, you want them small. Small as you can get them without ruining their quality. A quick fix to big file sizes is decrease the resolution or changing the file type. So, what I'm recommending is you use .webp files for images, .webm files for videos, and .mp3 files for audio (.ogg is smaller but not well supported).

Also...

Give your images alt text. Give your images alt text. Please give your images alt text. Accessibility is key, and it's needed for accessibility.
