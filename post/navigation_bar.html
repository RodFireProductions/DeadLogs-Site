How do you add/subtract links from the navigation bar?

So you wanna add extra navigation to your site or take some away? That's alright, but be aware that this may affect the mobile responsiveness of it.

In script.js find this snippet: 

let navi = [
    ['home','/index.html'],
    ['about','/about.html'],
    ['archive','/archive.html']
]

To add more links:

Add the name you want to show up in the navigation bar and the page's location relative to your primary folder in its own array below the others. 

let navi = [
    ['home','/index.html'],
    ['about','/about.html'],
    ['archive','/archive.html'],
    ['extra', '/extra.html'] // A new 'extra' link
]

To remove links:

Just pick the one you want to remove and delete it! 

let navi = [
    ['home','/index.html'],
    //['about','/about.html'] Has been commented out or "deleted"
    ['archive','/archive.html']
]
