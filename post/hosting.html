Hosting Services

  • Netlify
    • Free subdomain: *.netlify.app
    • Can use your custom domain(s) for free.
    • Can host multiple sites for free.
    • Hosts different frameworks.
    • Connects to git.

  • Vercel
    • Free subdomain: *.vercel.app
    • Can use your custom domain(s) for free.
    • Can host multiple sites for free.
    • Hosts different frameworks.
    • Connects to git.

  • GitHub Pages
    • Free subdomain: *.github.io
    • Can use your custom domain(s) for free.
    • Can host multiple sites for free.
    • Hosts static and Jekyll sites.
    • Connects to git (of course).
    If you choose GitHub, it'd be nice if you added the deadlogs tag to your repository.

  • Neocities
    • Free subdomain: *.neocities.org
    • You have to be a monthly supporter to use your custom domain.
    • Can host one site for free.
    • Hosts static sites only.
    • Doesn't connect to git. You can't update your site locally unless you're a supporter. (Makes me want to punch it, but you may not mind.)
    If you choose Neocities, it'd be nice if you added the deadlogs tag to your site.

    • Pros:

  • Freenom
    • Pros:
    • Can use a custom domain for free.
    Cons:
    • A lot.
      • TL;DR:
      • Unsafe and unreliable
      • Your domains can be taken away at anytime for any reason. (free plan)
      • They had their ICANN registrar accreditation suspended in 2015 for cybersquatting.
      • Bad customer service
    Don't use this one unless you're just needing a temporary domain for testing or something.
