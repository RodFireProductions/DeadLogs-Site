Welcome to the Comic Example!

This is an example page to showcase one of the DeadJournals comic themes. Instead of a comic though, I used two of my zines to kinda showcase how this theme functions.

The comic has extra nagivation abilities for when you're viewing on of the pages:

  • Advancing to the next page by clicking the page image;
  • Going back and forth between pages by using your arrow keys (<- and ->).
You can disable either, both, or neither of them at your own pleasure. Both are enabled in this example so feel free to check them out!
